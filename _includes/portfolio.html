Henro.org

A React/Next.js application that hosts interactive maps, guides, reviews, calculators, and pilgrim tools for pilgrimages around the world. I wrote my own mapping algorithms (elevations & distance calculations with route visualizations) + tiling optimizations with MapTiler/OSM.

The project has been featured in NHK, The Smithsonian Magazine, Thrillist, USA Today, AOL News, Commonwealth Magazine, and the Tim Ferriss Podcast. I was honored as a World Trails Ambassador of the World Trails Network in 2022 and lead the Technology Task Team from 2023-2025.
